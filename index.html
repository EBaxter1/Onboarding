Welcome to the Smash Bros at IIT

What is Super Smash Bros?

It is a series of crossover fighting games made by Nintendo. Currently the games are Super Smash Bros(Smash 64), Super Smash Bros Melee, Super Smash Bros Brawl, and Super Smash Bros. for Nintendo 3DS and Wii U(Smash 4) The goal of the game to to knock of opponents of stage.

Who are we?

Smash Bros at IIT is a student run organization on the campus of Illinois Institute of Technology. We provide a place for students to come relax and play video games. We have events for any level casual with open free-play set-ups and friendly tournaments at general body meetings or, for thoses who are more competitive, monthly in-school tournaments with prizes and bi-weekly buy-in touraments.
